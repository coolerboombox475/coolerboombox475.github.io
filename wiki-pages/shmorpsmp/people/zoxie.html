Zoxie is a citizen of Shmorperville. She is a bionuclear scientist who loves to experiment on anyone and anything. She's infamous for her experiments and is often seen as weird or off-putting by the other citizens, but that doesn't bother her.

APPEARANCE


Zoxie's fur is entirely white, and her horns are light blue. The light blue on her horns matches the blue on her attire. Her lab coat is mainly white.

PERSONALITY


Zoxie could be best described as nefarious. She's known for the various experiments she advertises to other residents, which only a few accept participating in. Various citizens feel as if she is hiding something, or that there's more to her than meets the eye. In the past, she used to be very paranoid, but she's gotten better at dealing with it. That doesn't mean it's entirely gone, though, and she's still hesitant to confide in others when they ask her for favors.

HISTORY


Zoxie used to work in a laboratory as a bionuclear scientist. Eventually, she started being irationally suspicious of her co-workers and as a result started to intentionally make mishaps. The mishaps gradually became more and more serious, until one day, she caused an accident so catastrophic she was fired. No one except Zoxie and her old co-workers know what the accident was.

POST-ACCIDENT


After the accident, Zoxie moved out of the city she lived in as she didn't have the funds to continue living there, and moved to Shmorperville. She still resides there to this day. Even after causing such a big accident, Zoxie is still spiteful and continues to use her knowledge for nefarious things. She is known for her experiments, almost all of which are deadly to participate in.

RELATIONSHIPS


LEGACYKIT
Zoxie has a sort of celebrity crush on Legacykit. Even though she's neutral, she does believe Legacykit's existence and believes they'd be the perfect couple.

RUBY
Ruby is Zoxie's best friend and scientist buddy. Often times they do experiments together and talk about the results with one another. They also play Touhou together a lot.

KIT
Kit is Zoxie's favorite experiment subject. They're immune to radiation and all kinds of poisoning which means they're perfect for experiments that'd otherwise be deadly. Zoxie finds Kit a bit endearing, especially their lack of knowledge about science and the questions they ask her whenever they're in the lab.

JASPER
Zoxie knows a bit about Jasper from the very little time she spent in the Nether. Otherwise, she's on good terms with him, but dislikes how he never seems to want to take part in her experiments.

IVORY
Similiar to Boombox, Zoxie's on good terms with Ivory. Ivory, like Boombox, also refuses to take part in her experiments, much to Zoxie's dismay.

RYAN
Zoxie doesn't interact with Ryan a lot, but from the interactions she's had, she doesn't see anything wrong with him.

SHOE
Zoxie finds Shoe suspicious as she doesn't understand why one would be so obsessed with trains and transportation. Isn't Shmorperville the perfect home? Why would someone be so obsessed with the idea of going away from it? There must be something they're hiding.

TRIVIA

  • Her favorite element is uranium. She loves working with it.
  • Her favorite planet and her second favorite element is Mercury.
  • She has a playlist.
  • She has 2 dogs, 3 cats, 3 llamas, 1 horse and 1 mooshroom.
  • She has a display of several objects she's created in her home.
  • She can recite the first 40 digits of pi from memory.
